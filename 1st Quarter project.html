Characters in Noli Me Tangere
Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin(Ibarra)
  • A wealthy young man of mixed Spanish and Filipino ancestry who has recently returned to the Philippines from Europe after spending seven years studying abroad.
  • Son of Don Rafael
  • He is well respected in the society
  • He is the main character of the story in the Noli Me tangere.
Maria Clara
  • Daughter of Captain Tiago and Doña Pía
  • A woman well-regarded in San Diego for her high social station.
  • Biologically Padre Damaso's daughter
  • Ibarra's Lover
Kapitan Tiyago
  • "Father of Maria Clara"
  • A Filipino socialite and well-respected member of the country’s wealthy elite.
  • A Filipino who wants to become a Spaniard
